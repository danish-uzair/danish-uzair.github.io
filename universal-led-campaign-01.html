Social Media Case Study

Universal LED Campaign 1

A premium social media campaign system created for LED signage promotion, combining product clarity, bold offer messaging, and strong visual hierarchy.

Project Overview

This case study presents a modular social media direction for Universal LED, designed to communicate signage services quickly while keeping the brand polished, confident, and easy to understand.

ClientUniversal LED
RoleGraphic Designer | Video Editor
DurationCampaign Sprint
CategorySocial Media Design

Challenge

The challenge was to make a technical signage service feel instantly understandable on fast-moving social feeds while keeping the visuals premium enough for business clients.

Solution

I designed a flexible post system with bold headings, clear product imagery, strong contrast, and repeatable layouts that can scale across offers, announcements, and lead-generation campaigns.
